Explore Nepal

Nepal is a beautiful country with breathtaking landscapes, rich culture, and ancient traditions. Here are the top 10 destinations you must visit:

  1. Pashupatinath Temple
  2. Pokhara
  3. Lumbini
  4. Mount Everest
  5. Chitwan National Park
  6. Annapurna Circuit
  7. Bhaktapur
  8. Swayambhunath Stupa
  9. Patan Durbar Square
  10. Bandipur

Pashupatinath Temple

Pashupatinath Temple is a UNESCO World Heritage site and one of the most sacred Hindu temples in Nepal.

Pokhara

Known as the gateway to the Himalayas, Pokhara is famous for its serene lakes and stunning mountain views.

Lumbini

Lumbini, the birthplace of Lord Buddha, is a sacred site for Buddhists around the world.

Mount Everest

Mount Everest, the highest peak in the world, is a dream destination for trekkers and climbers.

Chitwan National Park

Chitwan National Park is renowned for its wildlife, including Bengal tigers and one-horned rhinoceroses.

Annapurna Circuit

The Annapurna Circuit offers one of the most popular trekking routes in the Himalayas.

Bhaktapur

Bhaktapur, also known as the city of devotees, is rich in ancient art, culture, and history.

Swayambhunath Stupa

Also known as the Monkey Temple, Swayambhunath Stupa is a significant Buddhist pilgrimage site.

Patan Durbar Square

Patan Durbar Square is a stunning example of Newari architecture and artistry.

Bandipur

Bandipur is a charming hilltop town known for its preserved culture and breathtaking views.
